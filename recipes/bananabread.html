Banana Bread

Description

Super fluffy and delicious banana bread. The combination of banana flavor with chocolate goes so well together.

It also has a cinnamon crust for that extra layer of texture!

Ingredients

Dry Ingredients

  • 1+1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Wet Mixture

  • 3 large bananas
  • 3/4 cups white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted
  • Vanilla to taste

For the Cinnamon Crust

  • 50 gr sugar
  • 5 gr cinnamon
  • 60 gr flour
  • 50 gr butter

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven.
  2. Combine all dry ingredients in a bowl. Set them aside.
  3. Mash the bananas in another bowl.
  4. Add the rest of the ingredients for the wet mixture to the mashed banana and mix them well.
  5. Strain the dry ingredients into the wet mixture. Mix gently, just until everything is combined.
  6. Optional: You can add chocolate chips or nuts at this point to make it even tastier.
  7. Prepare your mold with butter and flour.
  8. Add your batter into the mold.
  9. Mix all the ingredients for the cinnamon crust in a bowl, until the mixture looks like sand.
  10. Pour the mixture on top of the batter.
  11. Bake until you insert a toothpick in the center and it comes out clean.
