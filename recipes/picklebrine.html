Pickle Brine

Description

Perfect brine for pickling your favorite vegetables! You can store them in the fridge for up to 2 months.

With this recipe you can make 2 jars.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup vinegar
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 3 tsp salt
  • Spices of your choice

Steps

  1. Stir together all ingredients in a pot and bring it to a boil.
  2. Remove from heat.
  3. Pour over your vegetables.
  4. Cover with a lid.
  5. Refrigerate when they're at room temperature.
